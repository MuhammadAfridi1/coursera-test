Personal information:

I was born in 2005 in Peshawar, Pakistan, a city with a profound historical legacy as one of the oldest continuously inhabited places in Asia. My roots trace back to the Afridi clan, one of the prominent Pashtun tribes from the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa region. Although I am still learning about my heritage, I feel a deep connection to the rich cultural traditions of my ancestors.
