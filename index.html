About Me

Born in Karachi, Pakistan, famed for biryani, not computers. From Barron's Model School to Govt Higher Secondary School, I pursued my dream of becoming a computer scientist. With numerous certificates from Coursera and edX in computer science, I now study at University of the Karachi. Driven by a vision, I aim to spearhead a technological revolution in my country, blending professionalism with a systematic approach. Determined to make a difference, I am poised to unify my aspirations into a singular pursuit of progress.
