Introduction

In addition to my formal education, I have undertaken various online courses, the details of which can be found in the Certification section. My language skills include complete command of English, Urdu, and Pashto, and I am at an upper-intermediate level in German. I am also proficient in Microsoft tools such as Word and Excel.

My experiences extend beyond academics. I have volunteered as a tutor, helping students with their studies. Additionally, I participated in football tournaments, though I chose to step away from the sport due to personal preferences.
